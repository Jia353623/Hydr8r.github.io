About Our Water Filter

We created this filter as a prototype for our gp project. in which we had to choose a environmental issue and implement a solution irl. Our issues was Water contamination in NCR.

what are our filters made of?

  • 1st compartment: gravel
  • 2nd compartment: activated carbon
  • 3rd compartment: PAC (poly-aluminium chloride)

Dontaions

We collaborated with an orginization, Nirbhed foundations. Our deal was if the filter works then the NGO will help assist us in mass production and spreading the filter to people in rural areas.
